Natural hair paintbrushes are the best choice for painting with watercolors, but they can also be costly. Sable hair is the most revered, especially Kolinsky Sable. The hairs are strong and when well-cared for, Kolinsky Sable brushes can last a lifetime. Red sable and pure sable paintbrushes are less expensive than Kolinsky Sable, but still quite good.
